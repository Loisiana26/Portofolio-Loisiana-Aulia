EduFarm Project Overview

EduFarm is an educational platform designed specifically for young farmers. Here, they can learn modern farming techniques, receive direct training from experts, and join a community of successful farmers who support each other.

Roles & Responsibilities

Hipster

As a hipster, I worked on the design of the login page and dashboard for the EduFarm platform.

Scrum Master

As a scrum master, I facilitated the Scrum process, encouraged collaboration, protected the team from external distractions, and ensured everyone understood their roles and responsibilities.
